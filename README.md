Privacy Policy for PictureViewer

Last Updated: June 1, 2026

We BurgeerGamez operates the PictureViewer application (the "Service"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your data.
1. Information We Collect and How We Use It

Because PictureViewer is a simple  image/ comics/ manga viewer, we minimize data collection to protect your privacy. However, to provide our core features, the app requires access to certain device permissions:

    Device Storage / Media Files: The app requires access to your device's internal storage or SD card to scan, catalog, and play your local audio files (MP3s, FLACs, etc.). This data remains entirely on your device and is never uploaded to our servers.

    Device Information & Crash Logs: If the app crashes, we may collect anonymous technical data (such as your device model, operating system version, and crash logs) via third-party services (like Firebase Crashlytics) to help us fix bugs and improve performance.

2. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal or device data to third parties. We only share anonymous, aggregated technical data with analytics and crash-reporting services to improve the application.
3. Data Retention and Security

    Local Data: Your music library data, playlists, and settings are stored locally on your device. Deleting the app will erase this data from your device.

    Online Data: The app may host a online service to help assist the app with such services like ai processing

    Security: We implement standard security measures to protect any anonymous data we process, but please remember that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take necessary actions.
